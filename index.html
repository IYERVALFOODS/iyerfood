About

Rooted in heritage, served with polish.

IYERVAL FOODS blends authentic recipes with a contemporary ordering experience, giving guests a clean, premium way to explore products, customise quantities, and place orders that stay safely stored.

This storefront is built for discovery and repeat ordering. Guests can filter items by category, manage quantities from a whoosh-style slide-in cart, and complete checkout with name, phone, address, and optional notes. Orders are stored locally so the experience remains fast and simple.
