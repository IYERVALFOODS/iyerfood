Our Story

Rooted in heritage, served with polish.

IYERVAL FOODS is born in Palakkad, Kerala. We bring you traditional recipes crafted with zero preservatives and uncompromised health.

Every product is made using age‑old family methods – sun‑dried vadam, hand‑pounded spices, and pickles fermented naturally. No artificial colours, no chemical preservatives, just pure, wholesome taste. Our small‑batch production ensures freshness in every bite.
